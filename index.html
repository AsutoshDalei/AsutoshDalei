Résumé

I'm Asutosh Dalei and I currently pursue a Master's degree in Data Science at the University of Maryland, College Park.

I'm presently interning at Nokia as an AI intern, where I am actively working on building a knowledge layer for Nokia's product documentations.

With over 2.5 years of industry experience in data science, I previously worked as a Data Scientist at Maruti Suzuki's R&D Division in Bengaluru. There, I contributed to projects that enhanced user experiences and operational efficiency, including developing conversational AI assistants and an AI-driven automobile health prediction system.

Work

Work Experience

  • Autonomous Network Intern at Nokia - Sunnyvale, California
    June 2025 – Present
    • Built a scalable retrieval system (“Knowledge Layer”) for Nokia product documentation, integrating vector, relational, object, and graph databases as a foundation for future RAG solutions.
    • Evaluated custom embedding and reranking models (Nomic, BGE M3, Mixedbread, ColBERT, etc.), improving retrieval relevance.

Past Projects
